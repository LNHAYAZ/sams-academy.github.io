Dr Shabbir Ahmed Mallick


Dr. Shabbir Ahmed Mallick, an esteemed figure in pediatric medicine with a profound focus on neonatology and metabolic disorders with emphasis on preventative healthcare system since 2 decades. Renowned for his expertise in tackling challenging cases, Dr. Mallick has dedicated his career to advancing pediatric care globally. With credentials including an MBBS from Dow, DCH from Dublin, MRCPCH from UK and FRCPI from Ireland. Dr. Mallick brings extensive experience to his role as a senior Consultant and assistant professor in Paediatrics.


Previously, Dr. Mallick served as the Head of the department at King Khalid Hospital in Tabuk for 15 years and later as the senior consultant of the CTU teaching unit at Sultan Qaboos University in Muscat, Oman.


In addition to his clinical pursuits, Dr. Mallick is actively involved in career counseling for medical and postgraduates, guiding aspiring healthcare professionals towards fulfilling careers. As the Pediatrics Residency Program Director, his leadership ensures the next generation of pediatricians receive comprehensive training and mentorship. Dr. Mallick's commitment to pediatric medicine extends beyond clinical practice. His advocacy for education and training is evident through his role as a pioneer in introducing preparatory courses for international pediatric examinations, such as the MRCPCH, in Pakistan more than a decade ago. With a diverse background spanning teaching and leadership roles in prestigious institutions across the globe, Dr. Mallick's contributions to the field of pediatric medicine are invaluable.
